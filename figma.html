Why City?
City Logistics is a full service local carrier, consolidator, and freight broker. We offer customized logistics solutions to the retail industry, focusing on fast and accurate consolidation, freight forwarding, and local delivery.
Each day our fleet of tractors and bobtails covers the entire Southern California market. Our 110,000 square foot distribution center is conveniently located within 30 minutes of the port and LAX.

Our services include:
LTL freight consolidation
Interstate freight brokerage for over-the-road and intermodal
Truckload same day and next day moves in the LA market
Pool distribution
Container transloads
Inbound/Outbound 3PL services including labeling, pick and pack, and storage
